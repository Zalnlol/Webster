Aptitude Exam

Exam Regulations

The Aptitude competition consists of 3 tests: General Knowledge, Math and Tech. It helps us to evaluate you in the most objective way about your thinking ability
Each test consists of 5 multiple-choice questions with an amount of time corresponding to the difficulty of each question. There are two types of questions: single-choice or multiple-choice questions. Candidates should carefully read the requirements of the question
The test may be monitored by the system and will be terminated if the candidate cheats during the test. Behaviors such as switching tabs, opening other tabs, minimizing tabs, etc. during the exam are considered violations. The system will end your test if this behavior is exceeded 3 times
